Methi Thepla
Ingredients:
  • Wheat flour
  • methi leaves
  • spices
  • curd
Steps:
  1. Knead dough with methi & spices
  2. Roll theplas
  3. Cook on tawa with oil
  4. Serve with pickle
Sprouts Salad

Ragi Dosa
Ingredients:
  • Ragi flour
  • rice flour
  • curd
  • salt
Steps:
  1. Make smooth batter with flours & curd
  2. Pour like dosa on tawa
  3. Cook both sides
  4. Serve with chutney
Oats Chilla
Ingredients:

  2. Pour like pancake
  3. Cook both sides
  4. Serve with green chutney
Quinoa Khichdi
Ingredients:

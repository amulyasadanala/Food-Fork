Dal Tadka + Jeera Rice
Ingredients:
  • Toor dal
  • Onion
  • tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ghee
  • Cumin
  • Rice
Steps:
  1. Cook dal
  2. Make tadka with ghee & spices
  3. Add to dal
  4. Cook rice with cumin seeds
  5. Serve
Paneer Butter Masala + Roti
Ingredients:
  • Paneer
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream, butter
  • Spices
  • Wheat flour
Steps:
  1. Cook tomato gravy with spices & cream
  2. Add paneer & simmer
  3. Make rotis from wheat dough
  4. Serve together.
Rajma Chawal
Ingredients:
  • Soaked rajma
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices
  • Rice
Steps:
  1. Pressure cook rajma
  2. Sauté onion-tomato-spices
  3. Mix with rajma & simmer
  4. Cook rice separately, serve.

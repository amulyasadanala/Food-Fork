Gajar ka Halwa
Ingredients:
  • Carrots
  • milk
  • sugar
  • ghee
  • nuts
Steps:
  1. Grate carrots
  2. Cook in milk till soft
  3. Add sugar & ghee
  4. Simmer till thick, garnish with nuts
Gulab Jamun
Ingredients:
  • Khoya or milk powder
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
Steps:
  1. Knead dough
  2. Make balls
  3. Fry golden
  4. Soak in sugar syrup
Kheer
Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Nuts
Steps:
  1. Boil milk
  2. Add rice & cook till creamy
  3. Add sugar & cardamom, garnish
Rasgulla
Ingredients:
  • Milk
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Water
Steps:
  1. Curdle milk
  2. Drain & knead paneer
  3. Make balls
  4. Cook in sugar syrup till spongy
Aam Ras
Ingredients:
  • Ripe mangoes
  • sugar
  • cardamom
Steps:
  1. Blend mango pulp with sugar & cardamom
  2. Chill & serve
Pumpkin Halwa
Ingredients:
  • Pumpkin
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom
Steps:
  1. Grate pumpkin
  2. Cook with ghee & sugar till soft
  3. Add cardamom
Dates & Nuts Ladoo
Ingredients:
  • Dates
  • almonds
  • cashews
  • ghee
Steps:
  1. Blend dates & nuts
  2. heat slightly with ghee
  3. shape into ladoos
Fruit Custard with Jaggery
Ingredients:
  • Milk
  • Custard powder
  • Jaggery
  • Mixed fruits
Steps:
  1. Make custard with milk & jaggery
  2. cool & mix with chopped fruits.
Dry Fruit Barfi
Ingredients:
  • Cashew
  • Almonds
  • Dates
  • Cardamom
Steps:
  1. Grind dry fruits
  2. Cook with ghee
  3. Spread on tray & cut into pieces

Chapati + Mixed Veg Curry

  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Carrot
  • Beans
  • Spices
Steps:
  1. Knead dough
  2. Cook rotis
  3. Sauté veggies with onion-tomato & spices for curry
  4. Serve hot and enjoy
Tandoori Roti + Dal Makhani
Ingredients:
  • Whole wheat
  • Black urad dal
  • Rajma
  • Cream
  • Butter
Steps:
  1. Soak & cook dals
  2. Sauté onion-tomato masala
  3. Simmer with cream
  4. Make tandoori rotis, serve
Lemon Rice + Papad
Ingredients:
  • Cooked rice
  • Lemon juice
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chili
  • Mustard
Steps:
  1. Temper curry leaves & chili
  2. Mix with rice & lemon juice
  3. Serve with papad.

Lauki Curry + Chapati
Ingredients:
  • Bottle gourd
  • Onion
  • Tomato

Methi Paratha + Curd
Ingredients:
  • Wheat flour
  • methi leaves
  • curd
  • spices
Steps:
  1. Mix methi & spices in dough
  2. Roll parathas
  3. Cook on tawa
  4. Serve with curd.
Moong Dal Cheela + Chutney
Ingredients:
  • Moong dal
  • Ginger
  • Chili
  • Coriander
Steps:
  1. Grind soaked dal with spices
  2. Spread batter on tawa
  3. Cook both sides
  4. Serve with chutney
Oats Vegetable Khichdi
Ingredients:
  • Oats
  • Moong dal
  • Veggies
  • Curry leaves
Steps:
  1. Roast oats
  2. Sauté veggies
  3. Add dal & water
  4. Cook till soft

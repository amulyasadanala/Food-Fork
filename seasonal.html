Mango Shrikhand with Poori
Ingredients:
  • Mango pulp
  • hung curd
  • sugar
  • cardamom
  • wheat flour
Steps:
  1. Mix mango pulp & curd with sugar & cardamom
  2. Make pooris from dough
  3. Fry & serve with shrikhand
Methi Thepla
Ingredients:
  • Wheat flour
  • methi leaves
  • spices
  • curd
Steps:
  1. Knead dough with methi & spices
  2. Roll theplas
  3. Cook on tawa with oil
  4. Serve with pickle
Baingan Bharta
Ingredients:
  • Eggplant
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chili
  • Onion
  • Spices
Steps:
  1. Roast eggplant
  2. Peel & mash
  3. Sauté onion & tomato
  4. Add spices & mash together
  5. Cook 5 mins
Pumpkin Sabzi with Roti
Ingredients:
  • Pumpkin cubes
  • Onion
  • Chili
  • Spices
  • Wheat flour
Steps:
  1. Sauté onion
  2. Add pumpkin & spices
  3. Cook till soft
  4. Make rotis & serve
Methi Paratha + Curd
Ingredients:
  • Wheat flour
  • methi leaves
  • curd
  • spices
Steps:
  1. Mix methi & spices in dough
  2. Roll parathas
  3. Cook on tawa
  4. Serve with curd.
Aam Ras
Ingredients:
  • Ripe mangoes
  • sugar
  • cardamom
Steps:
  1. Blend mango pulp with sugar & cardamom
  2. Chill & serve
Pumpkin Halwa
Ingredients:
  • Pumpkin
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom
Steps:
  1. Grate pumpkin
  2. Cook with ghee & sugar till soft
  3. Add cardamom

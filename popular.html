Poha
Ingredients:
  • Flattened rice
  • onion
  • green chili
  • curry leaves
  • peas
  • peanuts
  • turmeric
  • lemon.
Steps:
  1. Rinse poha
  2. Sauté onion & chili with curry leaves
  3. Add peas & peanuts, mix poha & turmeric
  4. Cook 3 mins, add lemon.
Idli & Sambar
Ingredients:
  • Idli batter
  • toor dal
  • tamarind
  • sambar powder
  • veggies
  • curry leaves
Steps:
  1. Steam idlis
  2. Boil dal
  3. Add tamarind & sambar powder with veggies
  4. Temper with curry leaves
  5. Serve hot.
Aloo Paratha with Curd
Ingredients:
  • Wheat dough
  • boiled potatoes
  • spices
  • coriander
  • ghee
Steps:
  1. Stuff dough with spiced potatoes
  2. Roll into paratha
  3. Cook on tawa with ghee
  4. Serve with curd & pickle
Dal Tadka + Jeera Rice
Ingredients:
  • Toor dal
  • Onion
  • tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ghee
  • Cumin
  • Rice
Steps:
  1. Cook dal
  2. Make tadka with ghee & spices
  3. Add to dal
  4. Cook rice with cumin seeds
  5. Serve
Paneer Butter Masala + Roti
Ingredients:
  • Paneer
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream, butter
  • Spices
  • Wheat flour
Steps:
  1. Cook tomato gravy with spices & cream
  2. Add paneer & simmer
  3. Make rotis from wheat dough
  4. Serve together.
Rajma Chawal
Ingredients:
  • Soaked rajma
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices
  • Rice
Steps:
  1. Pressure cook rajma
  2. Sauté onion-tomato-spices
  3. Mix with rajma & simmer
  4. Cook rice separately, serve.
Chapati + Mixed Veg Curry
Ingredients:
  • Wheat flour
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Carrot
  • Beans
  • Spices
Steps:
  1. Knead dough
  2. Cook rotis
  3. Sauté veggies with onion-tomato & spices for curry
  4. Serve hot and enjoy
Tandoori Roti + Dal Makhani
Ingredients:
  • Whole wheat
  • Black urad dal
  • Rajma
  • Cream
  • Butter
Steps:
  1. Soak & cook dals
  2. Sauté onion-tomato masala
  3. Simmer with cream
  4. Make tandoori rotis, serve
Lemon Rice + Papad
Ingredients:
  • Cooked rice
  • Lemon juice
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chili
  • Mustard
Steps:
  1. Temper curry leaves & chili
  2. Mix with rice & lemon juice
  3. Serve with papad.
Gajar ka Halwa
Ingredients:
  • Carrots
  • milk
  • sugar
  • ghee
  • nuts
Steps:
  1. Grate carrots
  2. Cook in milk till soft
  3. Add sugar & ghee
  4. Simmer till thick, garnish with nuts
Gulab Jamun
Ingredients:
  • Khoya or milk powder
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
Steps:
  1. Knead dough
  2. Make balls
  3. Fry golden
  4. Soak in sugar syrup
Kheer
Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Nuts
Steps:
  1. Boil milk
  2. Add rice & cook till creamy
  3. Add sugar & cardamom, garnish

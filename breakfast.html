Poha
Ingredients:
  • Flattened rice
  • onion
  • green chili
  • curry leaves
  • peas
  • peanuts
  • turmeric
  • lemon.
Steps:
  1. Rinse poha
  2. Sauté onion & chili with curry leaves
  3. Add peas & peanuts, mix poha & turmeric
  4. Cook 3 mins, add lemon.
Idli & Sambar
Ingredients:
  • Idli batter
  • toor dal
  • tamarind
  • sambar powder
  • veggies
  • curry leaves
Steps:
  1. Steam idlis
  2. Boil dal
  3. Add tamarind & sambar powder with veggies
  4. Temper with curry leaves
  5. Serve hot.
Aloo Paratha with Curd
Ingredients:
  • Wheat dough
  • boiled potatoes
  • spices
  • coriander
  • ghee
Steps:
  1. Stuff dough with spiced potatoes
  2. Roll into paratha
  3. Cook on tawa with ghee
  4. Serve with curd & pickle
Upma
Ingredients:
  • Semolina
  • onion
  • green chili
  • curry leaves
  • mustard seeds
  • water
  • lemon
Steps:
  1. Roast semolina
  2. Sauté onion & chili
  3. Add water & salt
  4. Slowly mix semolina
  5. Cook 3 mins and add lemon juice
Vegetable Sandwich
Ingredients:
  • Bread
  • butter
  • cucumber
  • tomato
  • onion
  • chutney
Steps:
  1. Spread butter & chutney
  2. Layer veggies
  3. Cover with bread
  4. Toast/grill
  5. Serve
Mango Shrikhand with Poori
Ingredients:
  • Mango pulp
  • hung curd
  • sugar
  • cardamom
  • wheat flour
Steps:
  1. Mix mango pulp & curd with sugar & cardamom
  2. Make pooris from dough
  3. Fry & serve with shrikhand
Methi Thepla
Ingredients:
  • Wheat flour
  • methi leaves
  • spices
  • curd
Steps:
  1. Knead dough with methi & spices
  2. Roll theplas
  3. Cook on tawa with oil
  4. Serve with pickle
Sprouts Salad
Ingredients:
  • Mixed sprouts
  • onion
  • tomato
  • cucumber
  • lemon
Steps:
  1. Mix sprouts with chopped veggies
  2. Add lemon juice & spices
  3. Toss & serve
Ragi Dosa
Ingredients:
  • Ragi flour
  • rice flour
  • curd
  • salt
Steps:
  1. Make smooth batter with flours & curd
  2. Pour like dosa on tawa
  3. Cook both sides
  4. Serve with chutney
Oats Chilla
Ingredients:
  • Oats flour
  • besan
  • onion
  • chili
  • spices
Steps:
  1. Mix all into batter
  2. Pour like pancake
  3. Cook both sides
  4. Serve with green chutney
